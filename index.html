Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about epoxy coatings and our services.

How long does epoxy flooring last?

With proper installation and maintenance, epoxy flooring can last 10-20 years in residential settings and 5-10 years in high-traffic commercial or industrial environments. Regular cleaning and avoiding harsh chemicals will extend its lifespan.

What are the advantages of epoxy flooring over traditional flooring?

Epoxy flooring offers numerous advantages including superior durability, chemical resistance, easy maintenance, seamless application, customizable aesthetics, and cost-effectiveness over time. It's also moisture-resistant and can be applied over existing concrete floors.

How long does it take to install epoxy flooring?

The installation process typically takes 3-7 days depending on the size of the area, condition of the existing floor, and the type of epoxy system being installed. This includes surface preparation, application of multiple coats, and curing time.

Is epoxy flooring suitable for outdoor applications?

Standard epoxy is not recommended for outdoor use as UV exposure can cause yellowing and degradation. However, we offer specialized UV-resistant polyaspartic and polyurethane coatings that are perfect for outdoor applications like patios, pool decks, and driveways.

How do I maintain epoxy floors?

Epoxy floors are very easy to maintain. Regular sweeping and occasional mopping with a pH-neutral cleaner is usually sufficient. Avoid harsh chemicals, abrasive cleaners, and steel wool. For commercial spaces, we recommend a maintenance schedule based on traffic levels.
